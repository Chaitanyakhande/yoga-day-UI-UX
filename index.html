About International Yoga Day

International Yoga Day, celebrated annually on June 21st, was established by the United Nations in 2014 following a proposal by Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi. This day, chosen for its significance as the longest day of the year in the Northern Hemisphere, aims to raise global awareness about the numerous benefits of practicing yoga.

Each year, the day is centered around a specific theme, such as "Yoga for Health" or "Yoga for Climate Action," encouraging people of all ages and backgrounds to incorporate yoga into their daily lives for improved health and tranquility.
